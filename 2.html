Linkedin Prospect List

Job Scope

Create a list of Marketing and Event management Contacts in sectors like
Information Technology ,Financial Services, Banking, Law Firms and etc
using LinkedIn. All the sectors should fall under the region US.

Client Need

Company Name, Industry, First Name, Last Name, Job Title, Email Address,
LinkedIn Profile, Address, Zip Code, State, City, Phone, Country.

Descriptions

LinkedIn premium account is needed to find all the Contacts from any
industry. The C-level contacts include CEO, President, Founder / Owner,
Chairman, Managing Director. By giving the type of industry with the
specific job title like CEO or Founder, it is possible to extract each
contact details in LinkedIn. It will result in their Company name, First
name, Last name, LinkedIn profile, Address, State, City, and Country.
Email address can be appended using company website, google search, etc.
